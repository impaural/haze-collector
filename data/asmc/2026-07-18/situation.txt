Isolated showers and cloudy conditions were observed over most parts of the ASEAN region today, except over the southern parts of Sumatra, the southern and eastern parts of Kalimantan, as well as Java and the Lesser Sunda Islands, where relatively drier conditions prevailed. Scattered hotspots were detected in Kalimantan. A few hotspots were also detected in Cambodia, Vietnam, Sumatra and Sulawasi. No significant smoke plumes were observed from the satellite imagery due to cloud cover.

Weather and Haze Outlook: Over the next few days, dry weather conditions are forecast to continue over the southern parts of Mekong sub-region, the southern parts of Kalimantan, as well as Java and the Lesser Sunda Islands, whereas shower activities are expected to prevail over the rest of the ASEAN region. The hotspots and smoke haze situation may escalate in areas experiencing prolonged periods of drier weather. The prevailing winds over the northern ASEAN region are forecast to blow mainly from the southwest to west, while southeasterly or southerly winds are expected over the southern ASEAN region.
