Showers were observed over many parts of the ASEAN region today, except over Java, the Lesser Sunda Islands, the southern parts of Sumatra, the southern, central and western parts of Kalimantan, and parts of the Mekong sub-region where it was relatively drier. Few to isolated hotspots were detected mainly in Java, the western and central parts of Kalimantan, the southern parts of Sumatra, Viet Nam, Cambodia and the central parts of Thailand. Slight to moderate smoke plumes were observed mainly in the western and southern parts of Kalimantan, and the eastern parts of Java.

Weather and Haze Outlook: In the coming days, showers are forecast over many parts of the ASEAN region, except for drier conditions over the Lesser Sunda Islands, parts of Java, the western and southern parts of Borneo, and parts of the Mekong sub-region. Isolated hotspots and localised smoke plumes may develop over drier areas. The prevailing winds over the northern ASEAN region are expected to blow mainly from the southwest or west. The prevailing winds over the southern ASEAN region are forecast to blow mainly from the southeast or south.
