Dry conditions were observed over the eastern and southern parts of the Mekong sub-region, and over the southern parts of Sumatra and parts of Java, Sulawesi and the Lesser Sunda Islands. A few to isolated hotspots were detected in parts of Cambodia, the southern parts of Viet Nam, the southern parts of Kalimantan as Sumatra, as well as in parts of Java. A moderate smoke plume was observed in the central parts of Cambodia and Viet Nam. Slight to moderate smoke haze was observed in the eastern parts of Java.The hotspot and haze situation could not be fully determined due to extensive cloud cover over the southern ASEAN region.

Weather and Haze Outlook: Showers are expected over many parts of the ASEAN region over the next few days, except for drier conditions forecast over Java, the Lesser Sunda Islands and the southern parts of Kalimantan. Isolated hotspots and localised smoke plumes can emerge in areas facing drier conditions. The prevailing winds over the southern ASEAN region are forecast to blow mainly from the southeast to south. The prevailing winds over the Mekong sub-region are forecast to blow from the southwest while winds over the Philippines are forecast to be light and variable in direction.
