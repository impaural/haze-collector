Wet weather and cloudy conditions were observed over most of the ASEAN region, except over Java, the Lesser Sunda Islands, the central and southern parts of Sumatra and parts of Kalimantan where drier conditions were observed. Few to isolated hotspots were detected in Java, Sumatra, Kalimantan, Cambodia and Viet Nam. Slight to moderate localised smoke plumes were observed to emanate from hotspots detected in the eastern parts of Java as well as the southern and eastern parts of Kalimantan. The hotspot and smoke haze situation over areas near the Equator cannot be fully determined due to cloud cover.

Weather and Haze Outlook: In the coming days, showers are expected over most of the ASEAN region, apart from Java, the central and southern parts of Kalimantan and the southern parts of Sumatra where it is forecast to be drier. Under prolonged dry conditions, the hotspot and smoke haze situation may escalate over fire-prone areas. The prevailing winds over the northern ASEAN region are likely to blow from the southwest to west. The prevailing winds over the southern ASEAN region are expected to blow from the southeast to south.
