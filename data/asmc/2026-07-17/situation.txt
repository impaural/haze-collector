Isolated showers were observed over many parts of the ASEAN region today, except for drier conditions observed over the southern parts of Sumatra, the southern and western parts of Kalimantan, as well as over parts of Java and Lesser Sunda Islands. A few hotspots were detected in parts of Viet Nam, Sumatra, Sulawesi, the eastern parts of Java as well as the western and southern parts of Kalimantan. Localised smoke plumes were observed in parts of Java, the southern parts of Sumatra and the western parts of Kalimantan.

Weather and Haze Outlook: Dry conditions are forecast for many parts of the ASEAN region in the coming days while showers are expected to continue over parts of the Philippines, Peninsular Malaysia, as well as the northern and central parts of Sumatra and Kalimantan. Isolated hotspots and localised smoke plumes can emerge in areas facing drier conditions. The prevailing winds over the northern ASEAN region are forecast to blow mainly from the southwest. The prevailing winds over the southern ASEAN region are forecast to blow from the southeast or south.
