Wet and cloudy conditions was present over most of the ASEAN region today, except over parts of the central Mekong sub-region where it was drier. Few to scattered hotspots were detected in Kalimantan and Sumatra while few hotspots were detected in Cambodia, Vietnam and Sulawasi. No significant smoke plumes was observed from satellite imagery.

Weather and Haze Outlook: In the next few days, wet weather is expected over most of the ASEAN region, with the exception of parts of southern Mekong sub-region, southern Borneo, Java and most parts of Lesser Sunda Islands. With that, isolated hotspots and smoke plumes may still develop in areas experiencing periods of drier weather. The prevailing winds over the northern ASEAN region are forecast to blow from the southwest to west. Prevailing winds over the southern ASEAN region are expected to blow primarily from the southeast to south, apart from Borneo where the winds are expected to be light and variable in direction.
